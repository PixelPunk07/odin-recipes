Sinigang na Baboy

Description:

Sinigang na baboy is a beloved Filipino soup known for its comforting sour broth made with tamarind. Packed with tender pork, fresh vegetables, and a vibrant tangy flavor, it's a hearty dish that perfectly balances savory and sour â€” a true staple in many Filipino homes.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1-1.5lbs (500-700g) pork belly, ribs, or shoulder
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 6-8 cups water
  • 1 packet tamarind soup mix (or fresh tamarind if preferred)

Vegetables:

  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1-2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 cup sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2-3 okra (optional)
  • 1 green chili (siling pansigang)

Seasoning:

  • Fish Sauce (patis), to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
